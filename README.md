## Why PayNest?

Most people track their bills across a dozen different places — a bank portal here, a utility site there, a sticky note on the fridge for good measure. Every month is a scramble to remember what's due, what's already paid, and what card is on file where.

PayNest replaces all of that with one intelligent payment hub that:

- Knows every bill you owe and exactly when it's due
- Lets you set autopay once and never think about it again
- Alerts you before anything goes overdue
- Shows you where your money is going — and where it's headed

---

## What you can do with PayNest

### 💳 All your payment methods, in one place
Add your credit cards, bank accounts, and digital wallets once. Set a default. Assign each bill to the method it should come from. Done.

### 🔗 Connect every recurring bill
Loans, rent, electricity, water, internet, insurance, subscriptions, school fees, traffic citations, or paying back a friend — if it shows up on a schedule, PayNest tracks it and keeps it organized.

### ⏱️ Autopay that works on your terms
Set the due day, the frequency (weekly, biweekly, monthly, quarterly, annually, or one-time), and how far in advance you want to be reminded. PayNest handles the schedule so you don't have to.

### 🔔 Never get caught off guard
A live notification feed surfaces upcoming and overdue payments before they become a problem. You'll always know what's due next — and never be surprised by a late fee again.

### 🤖 AI that actually understands your finances
PayNest's built-in AI goes beyond a simple summary. It spots unusual spending, flags cash-flow risks, forecasts your bills 6 months into the future, and answers plain-language questions like:

> *"When's my next rent payment?"*  
> *"What's my biggest recurring expense this month?"*  
> *"Am I at risk of running short before my next paycheck?"*
